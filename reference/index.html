About Us

Owner and operator Shaun Tully brings over 25 years of swimming pool construction experience to every project. Working with a team of dedicated staff, we help you to create the pool you want, the right shape, with the right features and the right equipment.

We pride ourselves on quality pool service and installation. If you're looking to remodel an existing pool, we do that too!

What Makes Us Unique

Quality, care, and the personal touch. You work with the owner to design and build the backyard oasis that is just right for your family.

Our Mission

Our goal is to build high quality pools to suit our clients' needs using the finest materials and methods available. To focus on the quality of each installation, we limit the number of pools we build in a season. We operate with a small, dedicated crew supervised by the owner, who is the head installer, working on-site, every day.

Services

Our Waterloo office at 550 Parkside Drive provides us a central location for our pool services and new pool installations. New Pools

Each pool installation is unique, and your pool will be designed with the features and accessories needed to create your perfect outdoor oasis. We start by listening to your needs, and develop a full plan for every element of your pool project. Our experience has shown that careful planning in the design stage makes the construction flow smoothly, leading to the timely completion of a well-crafted pool.

To construct your new Aquascapes pool, we use only the finest components from the best suppliers in the business. Our pool kits are from Kafko and Hayward supplies our circulation, filtration and control components. Aquascapes is committed to using the latest construction methods and techniques to ensure the highest quality and energy efficiency levels possible. Careful thought goes into equipment selection and plumbing design for each installation to provide the best water quality possible while using the least energy - keeping long-term operating costs low.
