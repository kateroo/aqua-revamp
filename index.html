About Us

Local, Experienced & Professional

The perfect choice for homeowners seeking quality pool construction in the Kitchener–Waterloo area.

Owner and operator Shaun Tully brings over 25 years of swimming pool construction experience to every project. Working with a team of dedicated staff, we help you create the pool you want — the right shape, with the right features and the right equipment.

We pride ourselves on quality pool service and installation. If you're looking to remodel an existing pool, we do that too! We specialize in vinyl-lined residential pool construction, guiding you "from Concept to Completion."

Our Mission

Our goal is to build high quality pools to suit our clients' needs using the finest materials and methods available. To focus on the quality of each installation, we limit the number of pools we build in a season. We operate with a small, dedicated crew supervised by the owner, who is the head installer, working on-site, every day.

Quality, care, and the personal touch — you work directly with the owner to design and build the backyard oasis that is just right for your family.
