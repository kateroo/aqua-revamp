# Weekly Maintenance Service

This service is for the regular maintenance of an operating swimming pool. Heavy spring clean up of pools is completed on an hourly basis since time and chemical requirements will vary.

## We will:

- Vacuum the pool
- Skim the pool
- Brush as required
- Backwash the filter
- Empty skimmer and pump baskets
- Clean cartridges as required *(additional charge)*
- Clean salt cells as required *(additional charge)*
- Test and adjust pool chemistry (chemicals invoiced monthly)
- Make sure equipment is operating well

## We will need:

- Access to the pool and all pool equipment and chemicals we have supplied
- Access to your operating water tap and garden hose

## You will need to:

- Maintain the water level of your pool
- Empty skimmer and pump baskets as required between visits
- Understand that on a windy or stormy day all our hard work can be undone shortly after we leave
- Contact us if there are any problems
