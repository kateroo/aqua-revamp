# Spring Pool Openings

## We will:

- Remove water and debris from your cover
- Brush tarps to clean as water is being removed
- Remove and roll cover and water bags if used
- Remove winterizing plugs from jets and skimmer
- Put jets, lights, ladders and other deck equipment back in place
- Prime your pump and start running (if water level is high enough)
- Add pool opening chemicals (salt is not added at opening)

## We will need:

- Access to the pool area and all equipment and parts that were removed for the winter
- Access to working outside tap and garden hose

## You will need to:

- Have your pool area clear and clean (pet waste) ready for us to work
- Fill the pool to the correct level if required
- Start the pump once the pool is filled if we couldn't because of low water level
- Start your regular water testing and chemistry maintenance
- Contact us if you have any problems

## Additional charge for:

- Waterfalls
- Deck Jets
- Solar System
- Spill Over Spa
- Double Cover

---

We do not service or start gas heaters because we are not certified. For service or maintenance of gas appliances we can connect you with the certified gas technician we work with.
