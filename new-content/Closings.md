# Fall Pool Closings

## We will:

- Lower pool level to just below circulation jets
- Remove pool jets, lights, ladders and other deck equipment
- Blow out all pool plumbing
- Remove all equipment drain plugs and cap from filter tank
- Install all winterizing plugs with foam rope for added protection
- Add pool closing chemicals
- Install winter cover and fill waterbags if used (additional waterbags required will be invoiced)

## We will need:

- Access to the pool area and all winterizing parts including cover
- Access to working outside tap and garden hose
- Access to electrical outlet

## You will need to:

- Have your pool clean, water balanced and pool area clear and clean (pet waste) for us to work
- Tell us if there is a pump timer we can't see
- Let us know about any leaking waterbags within 24 hours of service
- Contact us if you have any problems

## Additional charge(s) for:

- Waterfalls
- Deck Jets
- Solar System
- Spill Over Spa
- Double Cover
